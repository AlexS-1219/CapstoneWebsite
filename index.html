Pathogen Detection System Project

Welcome to the homepage of the Pathogen Detection System Project. Explore the sections to learn more about our work.

The technology responsible for identifying the micro-organisms is the AI powered captcha system. Essentially, it will look at slides of samples taken by the electrospray device and indentify the microbiles so that researchers can be more aware of what's out there.

This is the current leading technology for capturing samples for the AI detection system, others may be more useful though.

The Pilot Proposal tab takes advantage of an embedded map showing data that may be useful for deciding on which locations would be ideal for pilot testing.

The purpose of this site is to inform you on the advantages of using such technology, not only for public health but, pharmacutical companies may be interested in this data collection due to the profitablity of expanding research on active disease.
